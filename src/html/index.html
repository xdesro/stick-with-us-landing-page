what is this?

Stick With Us is a monthly release of sticker packs from independent artists. Most of each sale’s revenue goes to organizations like Planned Parenthood, the Transgender Law Center, and the ACLU. The next largest portion goes to the artist(s) who created the sticker pack, and we take a tiny cut to keep the lights on.
